About Chromancy

Chromancy is a gradient puzzle game, where you sort the colored tiles to create a smooth gradient between the locked squares

In the Options menu (click the gear), you can change the size of the puzzle (5x9, 7x12, or 16x19) and the difficulty of the puzzle (easy, medium, or difficult).
